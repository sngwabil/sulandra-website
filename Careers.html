Full job description
As a Direct Support Professional (DSP) with Sulandra Community Living Services LLC, you will support individuals in community settings with daily routines, skill-building, and participation in meaningful activities. Responsibilities may include: - Assisting with daily living activities and personal care (as applicable to the individual’s plan) - Supporting community involvement, appointments, and engagement - Maintaining a safe, respectful, and supportive environment - Documenting services and communicating with the care team Requirements and qualifications (may vary by assignment): - Caregiving experience preferred (training provided) - Ability to complete required onboarding and background checks - Reliable transportation and a valid driver’s license preferred
