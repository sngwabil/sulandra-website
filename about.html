About Us

Sulandra Community Living Services provides comprehensive home and community-based supports. We focus on outcomes that matter — safety, dignity, independence, and meaningful participation in everyday life.

Providing support where and when people need it.

Sulandra exists to serve individuals and families with consistent, respectful, person-centered services. Our work is grounded in listening first, planning carefully, and delivering support with professionalism.

We provide services that individuals and families rely on — community living supports, respite, life skills development, community access, and coordination that helps people stay connected to the resources that make daily life work.

Our approach is simple: build trust, maintain safety, and support progress in everyday routines. We measure success by stability at home, strengthened independence, and real-life participation in community activities.

Our promise: To show up with care, consistency, and accountability — every day.

How we work

  • Person-centered planning built around strengths and goals.
  • Reliable staffing, supervision, and clear service documentation.
  • Safety-focused support delivered with dignity and respect.
  • Family/guardian partnership with frequent communication.
  • Continuous improvement through feedback and outcome tracking.

Who we serve.

We support individuals with a wide range of needs — and we tailor services so that each person can live as independently as possible, in the setting that best fits their life.

Every service plan is developed with the individual (and family/guardian, when appropriate), and is built to be practical: meaningful routines, real skill development, and support that fits naturally into the person’s daily environment.

Support across settings

  • In-home support and community-based assistance
  • Group and supported living environments
  • Respite and relief support for families/caregivers
  • Life skills development and community access
  • Coordination that strengthens continuity of care
Approach

Person-centered planning

We plan services around real goals and measurable progress — not generic checklists. The result is support that feels personal, consistent, and empowering.

Partnership

Families and guardians

We collaborate closely with families/guardians, care teams, and community partners to ensure support remains aligned with what matters most.

We define impact through daily realities: a person learning a new skill, building relationships, improving independence, or maintaining stability through consistent support and safe environments.

Quality and Innovation.

Sulandra places quality first across operations. Our goal is to ensure every person we serve receives the right support — at the right time — in the safest and most appropriate environment possible.

Quality isn’t a single initiative — it’s the system behind consistent training, supervision, standards, documentation, and continuous improvement. We review outcomes, incorporate feedback, and refine service delivery so people receive dependable support that grows with them.

Quality Focus (expanded)

  • Clear goals with measurable progress and documented outcomes
  • Ongoing staff training, mentorship, and competency checks
  • Safety standards supported by supervision and accountability
  • Family/guardian feedback loops with follow-through
  • Incident review, learning, and prevention planning
  • Service audits to ensure consistency and compliance
Continuous improvement: We treat feedback as fuel — and we build stronger systems from it.

Culture.

Our culture is built around respect, empathy, and professionalism. We believe staff feel most successful when they are trained, supported, and part of a team that communicates clearly and leads with integrity.

Sulandra’s culture is “high standards + high support.” We expect excellence — and we invest in the people who deliver it, because great outcomes come from strong teams.

What we value internally

  • Consistent communication and follow-through
  • Training that prepares staff for real situations
  • Respectful teamwork and professional boundaries
  • Leadership that listens and problem-solves
  • Recognition for steady, quality work

Leadership.

Sulandra is led by professionals committed to ethical leadership, operational excellence, and person-centered care. Leadership exists to support the mission: stable services, strong teams, and outcomes that matter.

We believe trust is earned by doing the basics extremely well: transparency, accountability, training, and consistent communication with individuals, families, and staff.

Leadership focus

Accountability

Clear expectations, consistent supervision, and measurable service outcomes. We address problems early and build solutions that last.

Leadership focus

Support

We equip our teams with training, tools, and coaching so they can deliver safe and dependable support.

Community involvement.

We believe community inclusion is not optional — it’s essential. We help people access everyday experiences that bring purpose and connection: routines, relationships, recreation, volunteering, learning, and work.

Sulandra partners with local resources and community organizations to strengthen opportunities for individuals to participate in community life in ways that match their interests and goals.

Examples of involvement

  • Helping individuals build routines around community access
  • Supporting participation in events, recreation, and local programs
  • Connecting people to volunteering and meaningful activities
  • Building life skills through real-world practice
  • Encouraging natural relationships and social connection

Our people.

Our staff are the heart of Sulandra. Direct Support Professionals, coordinators, and leadership teams work together to provide stable services and respectful care. We look for people who are dependable, kind, and committed to doing the work the right way.

We invest in training and supervision so staff feel confident, prepared, and supported — and so individuals and families experience consistency and professionalism.

What you can expect from our team

  • Professional boundaries and respectful communication
  • Consistency in service delivery and scheduling
  • Support that prioritizes dignity, safety, and choice
  • Documented progress toward goals and outcomes
  • Collaboration with families/guardians and care teams
