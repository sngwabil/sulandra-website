Our mission and values.

We exist to support individuals and families with consistent, respectful, person-centered services. Everything we do is guided by safety, dignity, and outcomes that matter in everyday life.

Mission

Helping people live their best life.

Sulandra Community Living Services partners with individuals, families, and communities to deliver reliable, compassionate support. We focus on independence, inclusion, and meaningful daily routines.

Values

What we stand for.

  • Respect: honoring each person’s voice and choices.
  • Safety: consistent standards, training, and accountability.
  • Compassion: supportive relationships built on trust.
  • Inclusion: active participation in community life.
  • Excellence: measurable outcomes and continuous improvement.

Quality Focus

Sulandra is focused on people, quality, and outcomes across our services.

  • Person-centered planning with clear goals and measurable progress.
  • Ongoing staff training and supervision to support safe, consistent care.
  • Partnership with families/guardians and community resources.
  • Continuous improvement through feedback, reviews, and outcomes tracking.
  • Commitment to dignity, independence, and quality of life.
